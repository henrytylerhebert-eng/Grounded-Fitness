## Usage Notes

- Keep these files as source brand assets.
- For email campaigns, upload the selected logo to the email platform's image/content library and use the hosted image URL.
- For local HTML previews, relative paths from this repo are fine.
- Avoid stretching or recoloring the logo unless a separate approved variant is added.
- Public-facing copy should use Grounded Fitness / functional fitness language and avoid CrossFit-branded positioning.
