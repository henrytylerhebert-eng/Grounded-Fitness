## Marketing OS Anchor

Grounded Marketing OS exists to help Grounded communicate useful fitness guidance to members and, later, the local community.

The first operating engine is the Member Value Engine.

## Member Value Engine Anchor

The Member Value Engine turns weekly programming and attendance data into timely, useful, coaching-led member emails.

It should help members:

- Understand the week's training themes.
- Feel seen based on attendance patterns.
- Reconnect when they have drifted.
- Prepare for workouts in a practical, non-intimidating way.
- Build consistency without shame or pressure.

## Positioning Guardrail

Grounded is not marketed as CrossFit.

Approved language:

- Functional fitness.
- Coaching.
- Strength.
- Conditioning.
- Movement quality.
- Community.
- Longevity.
- Consistency.
- Real-life fitness.
- Training for life outside the gym.

Avoid:

- CrossFit branding.
- Box.
- WOD.
- RX.
- Hype-heavy fitness marketing.
- Gym-bro language.
- Elite fitness language.
- Beast mode.
- No excuses.
- Extreme transformation language.
- Health promises.
