# Wodify Sites Blog Audit

Source context: live Wodify Sites editor screens shared on June 12, 2026.

Purpose: document the website, blog, and personalization surfaces before treating Wodify Sites as part of the Grounded Marketing OS.

## Audit Boundary

This is an inventory and strategy document only.

Do not:

- Publish blog posts automatically.
- Republish the website automatically.
- Change website personalization rules without human approval.
- Connect site forms directly to a custom backend during the MVP.
- Use Wodify Sites personalization as a member-data or Wodify-record automation layer.

Grounded AI may draft blog strategy, article outlines, page copy, and personalization copy for human review.

## Blog Strategy Fit

Use the blog for local authority and trust-building content, not generic SEO filler.

Recommended content lanes:

- Functional fitness in Lafayette.
- Beginner-friendly strength training.
- What to expect in your first class.
- Group fitness versus training alone.
- Coaching, safety, consistency, and longevity.
- Movement quality for real life.
- Drop-in and free-trial preparation.
- Member education, not hype.

Avoid:

- CrossFit positioning.
- Gym-bro language.
- Unsupported health claims.
- Automated publishing.
- AI-generated posts without owner review.

## Build Your Own Rule Guidance

Yes, `Build your own` can be used, but only for public website personalization.

Good use cases:

- First-time visitor prompt for a free trial.
- Returning visitor prompt to take the next step.
- Mobile visitor call/text prompt.
- Campaign URL message for a specific landing page.
- Off-hours contact guidance.

Avoid:

- Creating Wodify leads directly from a custom rule without approved integration.
- Triggering member communication.
- Reading or using member/client data.
- Aggressive popups.
- Discounts or offers that have not been approved.

Suggested first rule:

```text
Audience: first-time website visitor.
Message: New here? Try a class at Grounded Fitness. Supportive coaching, real community, and a clear first step.
CTA: Book a Free Trial.
Destination: existing Wodify online sales/free-trial link.
```

## Conclusion

Wodify Sites should remain the publishing and website-execution surface.

Grounded AI should support:

1. Blog topic strategy.
2. Draft outlines and first drafts.
3. SEO/AEO briefs.
4. Brand-language checks.
5. Personalization copy drafts.

Grounded AI should not publish, republish, or automate Wodify Sites changes during the MVP.
