# Human Review Rules

Human review is required before any Grounded member email, lead message, or public content is sent or published.

## Required Review Checks

A human reviewer must check:

- Training themes accurately reflect the weekly programming.
- Member segments are based on real Wodify attendance data.
- Drafts match the intended segment.
- Tone feels like coaching, not marketing.
- The message avoids shame, pressure, hype, and gym-bro language.
- The message does not market Grounded as CrossFit.
- The message does not make health promises.
- The message does not diagnose members.
- The message does not imply guaranteed outcomes.
- The message includes only confirmed facts.

## Approval States

Use these review states:

- Drafted.
- Needs edits.
- Approved to send.
- Sent.
- Do not send.

## Required Reviewer Notes

For each reviewed draft, record:

- Reviewer name.
- Date reviewed.
- Segment.
- Approval state.
- Required edits.
- Risk flags.
- Final send decision.

## Hard Stop Conditions

Do not send if:

- Wodify data is missing or questionable.
- Segment criteria are unclear.
- The message contains a health promise.
- The message uses CrossFit branding.
- The message shames members for attendance.
- The message includes invented facts.
- No human has approved the draft.

## Future Publishing Review

For future public content, human review must also confirm:

- Local relevance to Lafayette or Acadiana.
- Accuracy of coaching advice.
- Fit with Grounded's public brand.
- No unsupported authority claims.
- Correct channel formatting.
