# AI Rules

## Allowed AI Tasks

AI may:

- Summarize weekly programming emails.
- Extract training themes.
- Classify programming by emphasis, such as strength, conditioning, skill, recovery, or longevity.
- Segment members from provided Wodify attendance data.
- Draft internal member emails for human review.
- Flag risky language.
- Flag retention risks.
- Recommend segment-specific coaching angles.
- Recommend follow-ups for human review.
- Recommend questions for human review.
- Summarize measurement records after a campaign.
- Surface recognition candidates.
- Identify milestone and anniversary opportunities.
- Flag attendance streaks and attendance drops.
- Suggest referral candidates for human review.
- Identify community story opportunities.
- Draft recognition content for review.

## Prohibited AI Tasks

AI may not:

- Send member emails without approval.
- Alter Wodify records.
- Make health promises.
- Diagnose members.
- Publish public content without review.
- Contact leads automatically.
- Contact members automatically.
- Create pressure-based or guilt-based messaging.
- Select Member of the Month.
- Publish recognition content.
- Ask for referrals automatically.
- Decide retention actions.
- Use personal stories publicly without human approval and member consent.
- Invent attendance trends.
- Invent member quotes or testimonials.
- Invent campaign results.
- Present missing metrics as success.
- Market Grounded as CrossFit.

## Drafting Standards

AI-generated member drafts must:

- Feel like coaching, not marketing.
- Be warm, practical, and specific.
- Avoid shame or guilt.
- Avoid hype.
- Avoid gym-bro language.
- Avoid CrossFit branding.
- Avoid medical claims.
- Invite members back into consistency without pressure.

## Required Disclaimers In Internal Use

Every AI-assisted output should identify:

- Source inputs used.
- Assumptions made.
- Unknowns.
- Human review required.

## Source Handling

If the weekly programming email is unavailable, AI must say `Unknown`.

If Wodify attendance data is unavailable, AI must say `Unknown`.

If engagement or performance data is unavailable, AI must say `No measurements found`.
