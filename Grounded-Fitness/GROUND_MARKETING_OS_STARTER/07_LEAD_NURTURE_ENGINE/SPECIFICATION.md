# Lead Nurture Engine Specification

Status: Roadmap only. Do not build yet.

## Purpose

Help prospective members learn about Grounded in a soft, informative, low-pressure way.

## Future Inputs

- Website leads.
- Wodify lead status.
- Lead source.
- Intro status.
- Referral source.
- Campaign source.
- Human notes.

## Future Outputs

- Intro follow-up drafts.
- Educational email sequences.
- Referral-source context.
- Objection handling.
- Low-pressure next-step prompts.

## Tone

Lead content should be:

- Soft.
- Helpful.
- Informative.
- Local.
- Human.
- Low-pressure.

Lead content should not be:

- Pushy.
- Urgent without cause.
- Hype-driven.
- Shame-based.
- Automated without review.

## Boundaries

AI may draft lead nurture content.

AI may not contact leads automatically.

Human approval is required before outreach.

Do not make health promises.

Do not diagnose.

Do not market Grounded as CrossFit.
