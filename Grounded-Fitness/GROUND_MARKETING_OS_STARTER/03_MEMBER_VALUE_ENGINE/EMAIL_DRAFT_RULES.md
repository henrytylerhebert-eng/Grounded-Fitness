# Email Draft Rules

## Good Member Emails Feel

- Human.
- Helpful.
- Encouraging.
- Coach-led.
- Grounded.
- Simple.
- Relational.

## Bad Member Emails Feel

- Salesy.
- Automated.
- Corporate.
- Guilt-based.
- Loud.
- Generic.

## Segment Guidance

### Consistent

Focus: recognition, leadership, recovery, intention, and sustaining consistency.

### Active

Focus: encouragement, weekly theme clarity, and next-step consistency.

### At Risk

Focus: low-pressure re-entry, personal support, and coach-led check-in.

### New

Focus: orientation, confidence, belonging, and early wins.

## Prohibited

- CrossFit positioning.
- Health promises.
- Diagnosis.
- Shame.
- Pressure.
- Guilt.
- Hype.
- Fake personalization.
