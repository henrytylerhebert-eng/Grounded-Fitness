# Community & Recognition Engine Specification

## Purpose

Strengthen belonging, recognition, referrals, retention, and community engagement.

This engine treats culture as an operating system. Some of the most important member communication is recognition, coaching, relationships, and staff follow-through.

Status: Phase 1.5 candidate. Not MVP.

## Inputs

### Wodify

- Attendance.
- Milestones.
- Anniversaries.
- PRs if tracked.
- Class participation.
- Consistency.

### Coaches

- Observations.
- Leadership.
- Attitude.
- Encouragement.
- Community contribution.

### Staff

- Nominations.
- Testimonials.
- Stories.

## Member Of The Month Workflow

Monthly trigger:

AI prepares:

- Top attendance candidates.
- Most improved candidates.
- Community impact candidates.
- Coach nominations.

Human selects winner.

Then system creates review-ready drafts for:

- Facebook post.
- Instagram post.
- Website story.
- Google Business Profile post.
- Email feature.
- Member spotlight video outline.

No draft is published without human approval.

## Member Milestone Engine

- 25 visits: coach notified.
- 50 visits: social post candidate.
- 100 visits: recognition candidate.
- 250 visits: major celebration.
- 500 visits: legacy member.
- 1 year membership: recognition opportunity.
- 2 year membership: recognition opportunity.
- 3 year membership: recognition opportunity.

## Staff & Coach Trigger System

- 3 visits: coach reminder to check in.
- 10 visits: coach reminder to celebrate progress.
- 30 days: coach reminder to schedule a conversation.
- Attendance drop: flag for coach-led outreach.

Outreach is not automated.

## Coach Content Trigger Engine

Potential stories:

- Member returned after injury.
- First pull-up.
- First push-up.
- Consistency streak.
- Weight-loss milestone.
- Confidence story.

Personal stories require human confirmation and member consent before public use.

## Community Calendar Engine

Potential events:

- Murph.
- Bring A Friend Week.
- Nutrition Challenge.
- Holiday Workouts.
- Member Appreciation Month.
- Community Service Event.

## Referral Trigger Engine

Referral requests should not go to everyone.

Look for:

- High attendance.
- Positive engagement.
- Testimonials.
- Milestone completion.
- Community contribution.
- Coach nomination.

AI may suggest referral candidates. A human decides whether and how to ask.

## Community Health Dashboard

Track:

- Member of the Month history.
- Milestone counts.
- Coach check-ins.
- Referrals.
- Anniversaries.
- Recognition opportunities.
- Attendance streaks.
