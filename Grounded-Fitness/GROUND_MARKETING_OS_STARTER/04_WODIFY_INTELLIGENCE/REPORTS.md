# Wodify Intelligence Reports

All reports are markdown outputs generated from fake/sample CSV data during the MVP.

Reports must separate facts from inferred recommendations.

Reports must clearly identify missing data.

Reports must not perform Wodify write actions.

## 1. Weekly Owner Brief

Purpose: Give leadership a plain-language weekly operating summary.

Should include:

- Member engagement changes.
- At-risk member summary.
- Lead pipeline snapshot.
- Revenue snapshot if sample data exists.
- Recognition opportunities.
- Recommended actions.
- Confidence level.
- Missing data.

## 2. Weekly Coach Brief

Purpose: Give coaches a useful staff-facing view of who needs attention.

Should include:

- Members to check in with.
- New member notes.
- Attendance drops.
- Milestones.
- Recognition opportunities.
- Suggested talking points.
- Missing data.

## 3. At-Risk Member Report

Purpose: Surface members who may need coach-led check-in.

Should include:

- Attendance decline.
- 0-1 visit members.
- Missed weeks.
- Previously consistent members now dropping.
- Confidence level.
- Recommended coach-led action.

## 4. Member Milestone Report

Purpose: Surface recognition opportunities.

Should include:

- Visit milestones.
- Membership anniversaries.
- Attendance streaks.
- Missing milestone data.
- Recognition candidates.

## 5. Member Of The Month Candidate Report

Purpose: Prepare candidate lists, not select a winner.

Should include:

- Top attendance candidates.
- Most improved candidates.
- Community impact candidates if nominations exist.
- Coach nomination notes if available.
- Missing data.
- Human selection required.

## 6. Lead Pipeline Snapshot

Purpose: Make Wodify lead data easier to interpret.

Should include:

- Lead count by status.
- Lead source notes.
- Intro appointment status if available.
- Stale leads.
- Conversion data if available.
- Missing data.

## 7. Draft Internal Member Emails

Purpose: Create review-ready communication drafts from report findings.

Should include:

- Draft for at-risk members.
- Draft for milestone recognition.
- Draft for general weekly member value.
- Human review required.
