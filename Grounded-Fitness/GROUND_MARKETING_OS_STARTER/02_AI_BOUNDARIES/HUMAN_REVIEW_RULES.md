# Human Review Rules

Human review is required before any member email, public post, referral ask, recognition decision, or business action.

Grounded AI does not perform Wodify write actions during the MVP. If a human chooses to create a Wodify task, tag, or communication after review, that action happens inside Wodify by a human.

## Review Checks

- Accuracy.
- Tone.
- Segment fit.
- No CrossFit positioning.
- No shame or pressure.
- No unsupported claims.
- No health promises.
- No diagnosis.
- No invented member facts.
- No public personal story without consent.
- Correct channel and audience.
- Facts are separated from inferred recommendations.
- Missing data is labeled.
- No Wodify write action is included.

## Approval States

- Drafted.
- Needs edits.
- Approved to send.
- Approved to publish.
- Sent.
- Published.
- Do not use.

## Hard Stops

Do not send or publish if:

- Source data is missing.
- Segment criteria are unclear.
- The message contains a health promise.
- The message diagnoses a member.
- The message uses CrossFit branding.
- The message shames members.
- The message includes invented facts.
- No human has approved it.
- Public personal details lack consent.
- The output attempts to create, update, delete, or alter Wodify records.
