# AI Rules

## AI May

- Summarize weekly programming emails.
- Extract weekly coaching themes.
- Classify programming emphasis.
- Segment members from provided Wodify exports.
- Produce owner-readable markdown reports.
- Produce coach-readable markdown briefs.
- Separate facts from inferred recommendations.
- Draft internal member emails.
- Recommend follow-ups for human review.
- Flag retention risks.
- Flag missing data.
- Surface recognition candidates.
- Identify milestone and anniversary opportunities.
- Suggest referral candidates.
- Identify community story opportunities.
- Draft public content for review.

## AI May Not

- Send emails without human review.
- Alter Wodify records.
- Create Wodify tasks, tags, communications, automations, memberships, payments, or lead status changes.
- Send emails, SMS, chat messages, announcements, or marketing emails.
- Schedule, publish, toggle, or configure Wodify communications.
- Create or publish Wodify email templates.
- Change Wodify communication settings.
- Automate Wodify in-app chat or calendar events.
- Convert leads to clients.
- Change memberships, holds, invoices, payments, or transactions.
- Publish public content without approval.
- Make health promises.
- Provide medical advice.
- Diagnose members.
- Create pressure-based or guilt-based messaging.
- Select Member of the Month.
- Decide retention actions.
- Ask for referrals automatically.
- Use personal stories publicly without human approval and member consent.
- Invent attendance trends.
- Invent testimonials.
- Invent campaign results.
- Invent revenue or retention outcomes.
- Market Grounded as CrossFit.

## Required Output Discipline

AI outputs must identify:

- Source inputs used.
- Confirmed facts.
- Inferred recommendations.
- Confidence level.
- Assumptions.
- Unknowns.
- Human review required.
- Missing data.

If a source is missing, say `Unknown`.

If measurements are missing, say `No measurements found`.
