# Naming Conventions

## Approved Brand Language

- Functional fitness.
- Coaching.
- Strength.
- Conditioning.
- Real-life fitness.
- Movement quality.
- Community.
- Longevity.
- Consistency.

## Prohibited Public Language

- CrossFit gym.
- Box.
- WOD.
- RX.
- Elite fitness.
- Beast mode.
- No excuses.

## Segment Names

- Consistent.
- Active.
- At Risk.
- New.

## Engine Names

- Member Value Engine.
- Wodify Intelligence / Analytics Engine.
- Community & Recognition Engine.
- Local Authority Engine.
- Lead Nurture Engine.

## Tone Rules

Member communication should be coach-led, grounded, useful, relational, and simple.

Lead content should be soft, informative, and low-pressure.

Public content should be local, human, educational, and authority-building.
