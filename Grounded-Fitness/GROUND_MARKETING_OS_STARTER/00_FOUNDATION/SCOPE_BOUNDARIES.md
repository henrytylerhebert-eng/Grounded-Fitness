# Scope Boundaries

## In MVP Scope

- Fake/sample Wodify CSV analysis.
- Owner-readable markdown reports.
- Weekly Owner Brief.
- Weekly Coach Brief.
- At-Risk Member Report.
- Member Milestone Report.
- Member of the Month Candidate Report.
- Lead Pipeline Snapshot.
- Draft internal member emails.
- Human review before any communication.

## Out Of MVP Scope

- Building a CRM.
- Building a membership system.
- Building attendance tracking.
- Building scheduling.
- Building payments.
- Building communications infrastructure.
- Replacing Wodify automations.
- Production Gmail integration.
- Production Wodify API integration.
- Workato API clients.
- Workato community connector installation.
- AI model connector integration through Workato.
- Automatic Wodify writes.
- Wodify task creation.
- Wodify tag creation.
- Wodify communication sending.
- Wodify communication settings changes.
- Wodify marketing email sending or scheduling.
- Wodify automated email toggles.
- Wodify template creation or publishing.
- Wodify in-app chat automation.
- Wodify calendar-event automation.
- Lead conversion.
- Membership changes.
- Payment changes.
- Full marketing automation.
- Public blog engine.
- Lead nurture engine.
- Local authority engine.
- Community recognition automation.
- Ad campaigns.
- YouTube automation.
- Canva automation.
- Google Business Profile posting.
- Financial automation.
- Automatic referral asks.
- Automatic coach outreach.
- Automatic workflow email sends.
- Automatic recognition publishing.

## Roadmap Boundary

Future roadmap modules may influence naming, records, and data design.

They may not expand the initial MVP.

The current MVP remains fake/sample CSV intelligence outputs only.

## Claims Boundary

If source data is missing, say `Unknown`.

If measurements are missing, say `No measurements found`.

Do not claim attendance, retention, revenue, lead conversion, or engagement success without source evidence.

## Wodify Boundary

Wodify remains the system of record for leads, clients, memberships, attendance, reservations, appointments, tasks, communications, automations, invoices, transactions, and reporting.

Grounded AI may read, analyze, summarize, recommend, and draft.

Grounded AI may not write to Wodify unless a future phase explicitly approves write actions.

## Recognition Boundary

AI may prepare recognition candidates, milestone reminders, referral candidates, and community story opportunities.

AI may not select winners, publish recognition content, ask for referrals automatically, decide retention actions, or use personal stories publicly without human approval and member consent.
