# Local Authority Engine Specification

Build local authority through educational, human, Lafayette/Acadiana-relevant public content.

## Content Principles

Public content should be:

- Local.
- Human.
- Educational.
- Practical.
- Authority-building.
- Low-hype.
- Reviewed before publishing.

## Future Content Categories

- Functional fitness education.
- Movement quality.
- Strength for real life.
- Conditioning for everyday capacity.
- Longevity.
- Member stories with consent.
- Community events.
- Local partnerships.
- Common questions.

## Boundaries

Do not publish automatically.

Do not make health promises.

Do not diagnose.

Do not market Grounded as CrossFit.

Do not use member stories without approval and consent.
